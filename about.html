About

Camille Carter

Camille Carter is an American poet and scholar whose work engages visual art, Black poetics, and literary criticism.

Her poems appear in national journals and anthologies, and her scholarship explores comparative literature and archival methods.

She is a PhD Candidate in Comparative Literature at the University at Buffalo.
